Exploring Outage Probability & Coverage

Configure Parameters: Adjust the transmitter power, frequency, and environment settings in the left panel.
Add Obstacles: Click on the grid to place obstacles that will block signal propagation.
  • Use "Normal" mode for typical buildings
  • Use "Heavy" mode for dense structures
  • Use "Erase" to remove obstacles
Analyze Results: Observe how obstacles affect signal strength and coverage:
  • Red-outlined cells indicate outage areas
  • Lightly shaded cells show shadowing effects
  • Hover over any cell to see detailed signal information
Experiment: Try different environments, obstacle patterns, and transmitter settings to understand how they affect outage probability.
